About Mike

I was born on October 31st, 1991 in Fridley, Minnesota. My family moved to Colorado when I was a toddler and for the most part it is where I have spent my life. I graduated from Chaparral High School and immediately entered the United States Navy as a Hospital Corpsman where I served for 3 years. After my service, I bounced around a myriad of professions. The coolest job I have ever had was being the News Director for 13 radio stations in the Big Horn Radio Network in Cody, Wyoming. Currently I work in Title Insurance for Fidelity National Title.

I have been a gamer for all of my life. My favorite games include Old School Runescape, World of Warcraft, and League of Legends. I enjoy reading about history. Specifically, I love looking back at the Roman Republic/Empire and to ancient Greece. Lately, I have been reading about the various stoic philosophers from those eras and trying to put some of their wisdom to use in the present. My favorite movie is The Shawshank Redemption. My favorite musician is NF.
